Our Mission

Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender and Queer. Over the past decade, LGBTQ people have gained more and more tolerance and acceptance in India. Nonetheless, most LGBTQ people in India remain closeted, fearing discrimination from their families, who might see homosexuality as shameful. Workplace equality is improving, but some employees, unfortunately, still face discrimination because of their sexual orientation and gender identity. People in the LGBTQ community are constantly fighting for equal rights and acceptance. Finding companies that include sexual orientation and gender identity in their non-discrimination policies is a way to identify companies that have made a public commitment to hiring more lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, and queer (LGBTQ) employees. Our mission at LGBTQ.me is to provide LGBTQ people with all the information regarding such job opportunities at the top-rated LGBTQ-friendly companies. We are committed to ensuring an inclusive environment for all employees. We also conduct orientations at companies all over India urging them and educating them about sexual orientation and gender identity thereby expanding our domains.
